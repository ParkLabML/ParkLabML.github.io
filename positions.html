Open Positions

We are looking for **postdocs** and **Ph.D.** students, who are creative and enthusiastic for research on privacy-preserving machine learning. Applicants should have prior research experiences in a quantitative discipline (eg., engineering, computer science, statistics, mathematics, and physics). Preferred qualifications include a high level of expertise in math and statistics, familiarity with Bayesian statistics, as well as experience with programming in Python (and some of deep learning tools).


To apply to a Postdoc position

Please submit the following:

(1) 1-2 page research statement

(2) a current CV

(3) the names and contact details of 2-3 references


To apply to a Ph.D. position

Please apply to the graduate program at the computer science department at UBC and indicate you want to work with me



Inquiries and applications may be addressed by email to Mi Jung Park (mijungi.p@gmail.com).
